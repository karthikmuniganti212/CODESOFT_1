Srushti Deshmukh IAS

☛ Srushti Jayanta Deshmukh is an IAS officer from the 2018 batch and a perfect example of beauty with a brain. The 27-year-old IAS Srishti Deshmukh hails from Kasturba Nagar in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh.

☛ This was her first attempt at the UPSC exam, and she successfully bagged the post of an IAS officer. She passed the CSE exam when she was 22 years old. Srushti has always excelled academically.It is truly inspirational to read about IAS Srishti Deshmukh.

☛ She has also been awarded an NCC A certificate. Through independent study using online test series and online study materials, she has earned this honour. This demonstrates her steadfast desire to represent the country as an IAS officer.

☛ In one of her interviews, she claimed that she has always aspired to be an IAS officer and has had this desire since she was a young child.

☛ She is a very down-to-earth and compassionate officer. In one of her videos, she sits with government school students and has a meal mid-day. She further instructed the caretaker to include vegetables in the diet and promised to look into fund allocations. This clearly represents her motivation to help poor children.

☛ Additionally, she has authored a book titled “The Answer Writing.” She goes into great detail regarding the Civil Service Mains Examination’s preparation and answers writing tactics in her book.

☛ Srushti claimed that she only intended to give the UPSC Examination one opportunity, and if she didn’t pass on her first try, she wouldn’t have attempted it again.

Srushti's Study Strategy



☛ Srushti was very consistent and she believes that, one of the main key to pass any exam is consistency. She even believes that without proper planning and consistency no one can achieve such difficult goals like becoming IAS or cracking any other UPSC examination.

☛ Studying one day for twelve hours and resting the other day won't help anyone crack IAS examination, rather studying for only five to six hours daily would put much more affect on any aspirants and this can also help to become consistent.

☛ Srushti strongly believes that there will always be someone as intelligent as you when you are preparing for any kind of government exams

☛ So to overcome your competitor you always need to be a step ahead and this can be easily achieved by taking online tests. Her primary source of study material was the internet. Internet is a huge help because it eliminates the need for specialized coaching in Delhi or at any other institute.

She wants to work towards improving society by focusing on women empowerment and education in India.Srushti Jayant Deshmukh really wants to work towards the betterment of society by focusing on the empowerment and education of women in India.

It was her dream, since childhood, to become an IAS officer, someday. She realized that she needs to become a part of the administrative system in the country. In that way, she would truly be able to help young girls who are forced into child marriage.

She has become a symbol for encouraging young people to exercise their right to vote under the Election Commission’s Systematic Voter’s Education and Electoral Participation (SVEEP) strategy.

She has motivated the entire section of youths and women in society to follow their dreams and ambitions and to be courageous.

She is an inspiration for everyone in the country.
